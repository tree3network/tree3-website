Tree3 products

Tree3 CLI

Introducing all-powerful Tree3 CLI

Use the Tree3 CLI, to control and manage your account and data. CLI allows you to publish decentralized ID and share content in a terminal. Tree3 is easy and secure way to share, sync and connect offchain data on Stellar Network.

Tree3 Platform

A decentralized content platform taking a novel approach

Using the Tree3 Platform, you can create your blog, promote your niche and set a goal to get crowdfunded in a unique way. On Tree3 Platform, you get various different rewards that are not based on votes but based on your content.
